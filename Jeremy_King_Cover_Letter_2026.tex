\lettercontent{Dear Mobius Digital,}

% (i) Who you are, what you want, and what you believe in.
\lettercontent{
My name is Jeremy King, and I'm a creative systems programmer looking to bring my experience to a team that is dedicated to pursuing their creative visions. I am very interested in the DevOps/Infrastructure and Creature/NPC AI positions at Mobius Digital.
}

% (ii) Transition
\lettercontent{
You wouldn't think it at first glance, but growing up I was a pretty earthy-crunchy kid. I remember playing Space Cadet Pinball and a handheld version of Tetris from time to time, but it wasn't until high school that I started getting really into video games. My first ``playthrough'' experiences came from a variety of popular indie games like Subnautica, The Witness, Celeste, and FEZ, so my introduction to video games was one beautiful, melancholic, or wondrous experience after another. I was quickly consumed by the burning desire to create evocative experiences and inspire others through games too.
}

% (iii). Skill & Qualification Match
\lettercontent{
Since then I have focused that drive on the technical aspects of software and game development. I have developed gameplay features and core systems for a variety of projects, including debug menus and tooling, room transition systems for 2D platformers, and a custom game engine written in C++. During my internship with Walmart Advanced Systems and Robotics, I gained DevOps experience by using Docker and Kubernetes to deploy a cloud solution that significantly reduced the setup and teardown times for end-to-end testers. During the seven week production phase of Leaves of Fate, I designed and developed underyling gameplay systems, led our gameplay design team, and oversaw Git workflows for four developers and ten asset contractors. I have also participated in several game jams and worked on interdisciplinary teams both large and small.
}

% (vi) Why do you want to work there?
\lettercontent{
Mobius Digital represents an opportunity to make my desire a reality. I, of course, have played Outer Wilds. I am the type that enjoys both the challenge of space exploration and xeno-archaelogical narrative. Outer Wilds is the closest I have come to reexperiencing the pure wonder of my first games, and that's the kind of creative vision I want to be a part of. I also want to contribute my time and technical expertise to a team that cares about each other as much as their projects, and I believe the culture at Mobius Digital reflects that. 
}

% (v) Conclusion
\lettercontent{
I feel that my passion for game development and technical experience with gameplay features and core systems make me a valuable addition to the team at Mobius Digital. I sincerely appreciate you taking the time to review my application, and I look forward to the opportunity to speak with you further.
}
